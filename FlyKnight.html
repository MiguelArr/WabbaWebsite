What is...
FlyKnight?



FlyKnight is a first person dungeon crawler inspired by niche games of olde tymes like Kings Field and Shadow Tower Abyss. It's slow, heavy, dark and challenging.f


Accompany the noble FlyKnights in their quest to vanquish the evil witch Lunamoth and defeat her minions to acquire new equipment and prevail.


Attempt this challenge solo or in a group of up to four players in online multiplayer, but be careful where you swing your weapons; your comrades might be in the way!


Features



  • Up to 4 players online coop action!
  • About 3 Hours of Gameplay through the main story
  • 11 Different Fish to catch and eat for huge buffs and bonuses
  • Retro style graphics and old-school gameplay
  • Use one of 4 types of weapon damage to DISMEMBER and HACK your enemies' limbs
  • Explore and Loot your way through many areas, make your build unique through the items you find!
  • You are slow and tire quickly, be mindful of your steps and attacks
Create your Fly!

Make your Fly unique with body type, head shape and skin/eye color options!

Wake up...

The humble FlyKnight wakes up from a rough night, with a splitting headache they raise from the ground, the campament around is in shambles and one thing is obvious: there has been a fight, a massacre.

Armed with courage and whatever can be plundered from the already cold corpses of your fallen comrades, you will venture further and end your quest.

Summon...
your Friends!

Up to 3 more players can join you on your quest in a public or private lobby. Every character progresses at the same time and the journey can be shared from start to finish.

Specs

FlyKnight doesn't use textures, dynamic lighting or fancy modern effects like Lumen, RTX or Nanite, but it was made in the Unreal Engine which isn't exactly lightweight.

Some areas of the game can be a bit heavy on the CPU due to high amounts of enemies, but it's rare to see it dip below 30FPS in older computers.

That said, many users have reported crashes when interacting with lootable items in computers that use very old GPUs and/or intel ark GPUs, a potential fix for this issue can be found Here.
