Welcome to Salguro's Castle...

Prepare yourself!

In an attempt to escape from poverty, Melinda infiltrated Salguro's Castle. Now, she must escape it.

Sorceress is a magic-based immersive sim where you'll have to become a dual-wielding agile spellcaster and battle the minions and terrors that prowl a hand-crafted sprawling castle where unrestricted experiments have given birth to unrelenting threats...

Sorceress had a successful Kickstarter campaign in 2023 and it's currently deep in development, for updates and announcements Wishlist and Follow on Steam.

In this Experience you will:

  • Cast elemental magic and mix it to cause chain reactions like electrifying water, freezing a bubble, dousing fire, igniting oil, activate machinery, and more!
  • Kick your enemies, kick environment objects, even kick a surface to perform a wall-jump!
  • Improvise with the many tools at your disposal, steal your enemies' weapons to use them or throw them back, push them towards hazards or toss objects at them
  • Explore the innards of Salguro's Castle, a hand-crafted sprawling environment full of verticality and platforming opportunities
  • Obtain new weapons, boots, hats and more to customize your loadout
  • Unveil the evil plots of The Sulgeri Witches and get ready to suffer the products of their experiments
