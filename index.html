WELCOME

Sup, I'm Wabbaboy, I make games and stuff. I was born on 1993 and live in a cozy town on the eastern side of Spain from which I'm a native.

I'm a game developer, graduated in a 3D game art course in 2015, had a job here and there and then began making my own games as a self-taught programmer.
My main project is Sorceress, a game where you step into the boots of a young gal that casts spells and kicks stuff, it's rad I think.

Currently I'm making FlyKnight, a King's Field inspired game with Runescape-style graphics, originally it was made in two weeks as part of a friendly gamejam, but I've decided to expand on it to unwind from my main project and gain a more stable money situation.

Also, hi! Welcome to the site, I made this place mostly to self indulge and have fun learning HTML, also I have several interests and hobbies I want to talk about, hope you enjoy your time in here~
