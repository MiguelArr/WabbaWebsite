Sup, I'm Wabbaboy, some guy from Spain that makes games and stuff. I was born on 1993 and live in a cozy town on the eastern side of Spain.

I'm a game developer, graduated in a 3D game art course in 2015, had a job here and there and then begun making my own games as a self-taught programmer. My main project is Sorceress, a game where you step into the boots of a young gal that casts spells and kicks stuff, it's rad I think.

I also made FlyKnight, a King's Field inspired game with Runescape-style graphics which was made in only 2 weeks with help from very talented people.

I have some other interests and I've made this website to talk about them, learn HTML and indulge myself a little bit, hope you enjoy your time in here.
